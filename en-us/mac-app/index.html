Coming soon

Record Picker for Mac is in preparation.

The same collection, a larger workspace, and a Mac interface built for record collectors.

Get the iPhone and iPad appExplore current features

The Xcode project now includes a dedicated macOS target alongside the iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch apps. The Mac app is being prepared as its own native experience, not as a quick stretched phone view.

Record Picker already keeps the crate, wishlist, resolved duplicates, exclusions, history, custom covers, and backups in sync across Apple devices. The Mac version is the natural place to make the larger collection workflows feel calmer and faster.

Built for a larger library view

Browsing a crate, checking metadata, spotting duplicates, and cleaning a catalog are all easier when the interface can breathe. The Mac app is being shaped around that extra space.

Designed for keyboard and trackpad

The goal is a Mac workflow that feels natural from the first click: quick navigation, precise edits, and a comfortable way to choose the next record without leaving the desk.

Connected to the same collection

iCloud sync is already central to Record Picker. The Mac app is intended to work with the same crate and wishlist you use on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

What the Mac app is being designed for

  • Browsing and filtering a larger vinyl crate with less friction.
  • Reviewing data quality, missing metadata, duplicates, and custom covers.
  • Managing imports, exports, backups, wishlist items, and collection cleanup.
  • Keeping the draw experience close to the iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch versions.

Want to follow the Mac version?

The iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch app is available now. The Mac version is still in preparation.
