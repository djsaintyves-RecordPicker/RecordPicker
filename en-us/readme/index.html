Record Picker v1.2

Record Picker brings together cataloging, smart picking, data enrichment, statistics, backups, and system companions to bring your music collection back to life.

The page below presents the main features in version 1.2, from the first import to the next record pick, including artwork, filters, data quality checks, and export tools.

Features

Complete music catalog

Build a usable collection, even when the data you start from is incomplete.

  • CSV collection import
  • Manual vinyl, CD, and album entry
  • Barcode scanning with the camera
  • Metadata lookup with MusicBrainz and Discogs
  • Editable record detail sheets for completing information

Artwork and visual data

Keep the collection pleasant to browse with artwork you retrieve or choose yourself.

  • Artwork lookup through Cover Art Archive
  • Manual import from Photos or Files
  • Web lookup when artwork is missing
  • Restore original artwork
  • Quality checks for missing artwork and data

Smart picking

Choose the next record without losing control over what can be drawn from the bin.

  • Animated random draw
  • Filtering by years, genres, styles, favorites, and availability
  • Same-artist exclusion
  • Favor favorites or limit picks to favorites
  • Temporary exclusions for records, artists, or criteria

Moods and playback

Record Picker can steer choices by mood and keep a trail of what you play.

  • Mood-based picking with local Apple models when available
  • Local metadata-based matching when models are unavailable
  • Recent history of drawn or played records
  • Playback through Apple Music when available
  • Wishlist for keeping wanted records close at hand

Browsing and collection insight

Browse, check, and understand your collection without leaving the app.

  • Record bin with search, sorting, and filters
  • Views adapted for iPhone and iPad
  • Large covers and fast lists
  • Collection, draw, favorite, and exclusion statistics
  • Detection of missing tracks, formats, labels, dates, and data

Backup, automation, and privacy

Advanced tools stay under your control, with the collection stored locally.

  • Collection backup and restore
  • Readable CSV and JSON exports
  • Siri Shortcuts and system actions
  • Apple Watch companion
  • No user account, no advertising, and no Record Picker server storing your collection
