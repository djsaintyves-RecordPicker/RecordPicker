Vinyl collection

Manage and rediscover a vinyl collection

A useful collection is not only organised. It stays alive.

App StoreView screenshots

Cataloging a vinyl collection is not only about knowing what you own. It is about finding, choosing, correcting, backing up, and rediscovering records that can otherwise disappear on the shelf.

Record Picker combines a readable record bin, editable data, metadata lookups, artwork, statistics, and smart picking to keep the collection active.

Import, add, correct

A collection rarely starts with perfect data. Record Picker supports CSV import, manual entry, and barcode scanning so you can begin quickly.

Editable record sheets then let you complete dates, formats, genres, styles, labels, tracks, and notes.

Enrich without losing control

MusicBrainz, Discogs, and Cover Art Archive lookups run only when you start them. Artwork can also be chosen manually from Photos, Files, or the web.

This keeps the collection local while giving you tools to improve data quality over time.

Rediscover through statistics and picking

Statistics show favorites, draws, exclusions, and missing data. They give you another way to read the collection.

Random picks, moods, and history then put records back into circulation, especially the ones you forget without noticing.

Key takeaways

  • Import or enter the collection.
  • Complete metadata and artwork.
  • Back up and export regularly.
  • Use filters, statistics, and draws to rediscover albums.
