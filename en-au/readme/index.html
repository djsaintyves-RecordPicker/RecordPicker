Record Picker v1.4

Record Picker brings together fair draw, cover swipes, iPhone landscape selection, enriched cataloging, iCloud, export, 29 languages, and a reimagined Apple Watch app to bring your music collection back to life.

The page below presents the main features in version 1.4, from iPhone landscape selection to cover swipes, including Apple Watch, Discogs, iCloud, languages, and privacy.

Features

A well-kept collection

Build a clean, checkable, pleasant collection, even when public databases are not enough.

  • Quick entry via barcode scan
  • Metadata from MusicBrainz and Discogs
  • When MusicBrainz does not know the record, Discogs takes over automatically
  • Wishlist separate from your crate
  • Duplicate detection, exclusions, and browsable history

Artwork and visual data

Keep the collection pleasant to browse with artwork you retrieve or choose yourself.

  • Artwork lookup through Cover Art Archive
  • Manual import from Photos or Files
  • Web lookup when artwork is missing
  • Restore original artwork
  • Custom covers when public databases are not enough

iPhone landscape selector

Turn iPhone sideways to choose with the cover, metadata, and controls visible at the same time.

  • Cover on the left and full details on the right
  • Title, artist, genre tags, format, label, and added-in year visible
  • Pick button centered next to metadata
  • Floating bottom toolbar balanced between the cover and screen edge
  • Statistics reflow into three columns in landscape

Fair draw

Choose the next record without losing control, with randomness that keeps the collection moving.

  • Weighted draw that favors less-played records
  • Filters by year, genre, format, speed, and favorites
  • Temporary exclusions to set aside without deleting
  • Swipe the cover left to draw a new record
  • Swipe right to undo the last draw, also on iPad
  • Browsable history to understand what keeps coming back

Moods and playback

Choose by mood and keep a listening trail so the collection keeps revealing itself over time.

  • Mood-based picking with local Apple models when available
  • Local metadata-based matching when models are unavailable
  • Narrative statistics with on-device Apple Intelligence when available
  • Recent history of drawn or played records
  • Playback through Apple Music, Spotify, Deezer, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, SoundCloud, Qobuz, and Tidal when available

Apple Watch reimagined

Version 1.3 transforms the wrist experience with an interface designed for quick gestures.

  • Cover shown as a blurred backdrop
  • Favorite, undo, and next draw buttons within thumb's reach
  • Dedicated haptic feedback for each action
  • Layout adapted from 41 mm to Ultra
  • Faster iPhone-Watch sync

Browsing and collection insight

Browse, check, and understand your collection without leaving the app, with distinct iPhone and iPad interfaces.

  • Quick navigation through the crate
  • Small red star on every iPhone row and iPad tile to mark or unmark a favorite
  • Unified Liquid Glass buttons
  • Readable iPad rows and direct access to details from covers
  • iPad drag and drop where it actually helps
  • Duplicate detection and data quality checks
  • Data quality checks for missing years, added dates, duplicates, labels, artwork, genres, formats, and tracks
  • Genre, style, and track completion through MusicBrainz and then Discogs
  • Swipe-to-delete in wishlist and AI Mood history

iCloud, export, and privacy

Your data follows your devices through iCloud, without third-party AI providers or cloud AI.

  • Crate, wishlist, resolved duplicates, exclusions, history, and restorable deletions synced through iCloud
  • Custom covers synced across devices
  • CSV for spreadsheets and clean JSON, separated for crate and wishlist
  • Full backup on demand
  • No third-party AI providers, no cloud AI; Apple Intelligence stays on-device
  • Internet reviews are never scraped automatically

29 languages

Record Picker speaks yours, from English to Japanese, from Arabic to Korean, from Hebrew to Turkish.

  • New languages and variants in 1.3
  • English for Australia, Canada, United Kingdom, and United States
  • French for France and Canada
  • Portuguese for Brazil and Portugal
  • Arabic, Hebrew, Hindi, Japanese, Korean, Simplified and Traditional Chinese

App Store history

v1.4

iPhone landscape, cover swipes, and faster favorites

June 23, 2026

  • iPhone landscape selector: turn the phone sideways to see the cover on the left and full record details on the right, including title, artist, genre tags, format, label, and added-in year.
  • The Pick button stays centered next to the metadata, while the bottom toolbar floats equidistantly between the cover and the screen edge.
  • Swipe the album cover from right to left to draw a new record, or left to right to undo the last draw. Tap still opens details, long-press still excludes, and it works on iPad too.
  • In the record crate, the Favorite chip is replaced by a small red star on every iPhone row and every iPad grid tile: one tap to mark, one tap to unmark.
  • Statistics get denser in landscape: on iPhone, tiles reflow into three columns, matching the iPad density.
  • Cleaner swipes everywhere: swipe-to-delete is back on the wishlist and added to AI Mood history; old cross-screen swipes that fought row-level deletions have been retired.
v1.3

Apple Watch reimagined, Discogs fallback, and 29 languages

June 22, 2026

  • Apple Watch reimagined: the cover sits as a blurred backdrop, with three thumb-friendly buttons for favorite, undo the last draw, and next draw, each with dedicated haptic feedback.
  • Apple Watch layout adapted from 41 mm to Ultra.
  • Barcode scan now falls back to Discogs when MusicBrainz does not know the reference; if Discogs finds the edition, the form is pre-filled automatically.
  • Record Picker is available in 29 languages, with new variants and localizations: Arabic, Catalan, Korean, Danish, English for Australia/Canada/United Kingdom, Finnish, Canadian French, Hebrew, Hindi, Indonesian, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese for Brazil/Portugal, Russian, Swedish, and Turkish.
  • Small polish: better balanced cover picker sheet on iPad, faster iPhone-Watch sync, and a discreet App Store review request after regular use.
v1.2

  • Full music collection catalogue for imports, manual album entry and barcode scanning.
  • Animated random draw, year filters, favourites, temporary exclusions, listening history and collection statistics.
  • Mood-based picking with local Apple models when available, otherwise on-device matching from collection metadata.
  • MusicBrainz metadata lookup, Cover Art Archive artwork or manual artwork import, backup/restore, Siri Shortcuts and Apple Watch companion.
  • The collection stays stored locally; metadata and artwork lookups happen only when the user starts them.
