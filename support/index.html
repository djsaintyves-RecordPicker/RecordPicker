RecordPicker Support

RecordPicker helps you manage and rediscover your physical music collection, including vinyl records, CDs, and albums.

What RecordPicker helps with

  • Importing a collection from CSV
  • Adding albums manually
  • Scanning barcodes
  • Searching metadata and cover artwork
  • Using random draw and mood-based suggestions
  • Managing favorites, exclusions, history, backups, and Apple Watch sync

Contact

For support, feature requests, or privacy questions, contact djsaintyves@mac.com.
